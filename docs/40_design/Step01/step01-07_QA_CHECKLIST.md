# 07. QA Checklist

## 1. Brand Identity
- [ ] Is "Built with GeneratePress" hidden in the footer?
- [ ] Is the site title/logo legible and professional?
- [ ] Does the overall tone feel "premium financial editorial" (restrained luxury)?

## 2. Navigation & Shell
- [ ] Is the header/nav clear and well-separated from the content?
- [ ] Is the footer rhythm calm and professional?
- [ ] Are hover/focus states visible but subtle?

## 3. Single-Post Experience
- [ ] Is the H1-H3 hierarchy clear and aesthetically pleasing?
- [ ] Is paragraph line-height between 1.8 and 2.0?
- [ ] Is inter-paragraph spacing consistent?
- [ ] are Blockquotes visually distinct but restrained?

## 4. Components
- [ ] Are Analytical Badges stylized as pills/labels (not just bracketed text)?
- [ ] Is the "Picks" box background warm and trustworthy?
- [ ] Is the "Source" box readable and "institutional"?

## 5. Archive & Consistency
- [ ] Do Archive/Category pages follow the same rhythm as the single post shell?
- [ ] Are excerpts readable without clutter?

## 6. Layout & Responsiveness
- [ ] Desktop (1280px+): Content centered, no excessive width.
- [ ] Mobile (375px & 390px): No horizontal overflow.
- [ ] Mobile: Navigation hamburger is functional.
- [ ] Mobile: Typography remains legible and comfortable.

## 7. Safety & Regression
- [ ] verify `generator.py` remains untouched.
- [ ] verify `styles/tokens.py` remains untouched.
- [ ] verify existing content renders correctly.
